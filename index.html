Washington Square Park in the early morning is different than it is later in the day.
As the day progresses, this park becomes a destination for people, but right now, it's
just part of the morning commute. The majority of people here at 8AM are not sitting and
enjoying the morning, but instead walking through, sometimes without even looking up.
The most beautiful part about Washington Square Park at this time of day is how silent
it is. The rest of the city seems so far away, and all you can hear are the quiet voices
and movements of other people, the occasional barking dog, and the wind.
